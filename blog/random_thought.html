Random Thought

Published: April 4, 2025

I think one of the most underrated skills as a data scientist is storytelling.

We spend a lot of time learning technical skills — machine learning, SQL, data engineering — but at the end of the day, you need to convince someone your work matters.

I’ve learned that how you present your analysis often matters more than what’s in it. You don’t need to use fancy words — just guide the reader like they’re on a journey with you. Why did you start? What did you expect? What surprised you? What do you recommend?

“The goal is to simplify without dumbing down.”

Just a quick thought today. Keep writing. Keep thinking.
